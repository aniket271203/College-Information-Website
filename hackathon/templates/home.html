Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for a student or faculty member?

To search for a student, visit the Students page by accessing that section in the navigation bar. You will find a search bar in the Students page through which you can search for a student. To search for a faculty, access the faculty page and rest of the process is same.

Can I update my contact information in the directory?

No, you cannot change any data unless you have an Authentication key. However, if you are an authority and have an authentication key, you can insert, edit or delete any information.

Are my courses updated from time to time?

Yes, the courses of every student are updated from time to time by the authorities.
